About us

Fagrimacs is a digital platform enable farmers to easy rent-out and rent-in their tractor and farming equipment from nearby farmer.
The platform ensures fast and easily availability of tractor or implements and on-time farming solution such as soil cultivation, planting, fertilizing & pest control, Drainage & Irrigation, loading, harvesting & post-harvesting and Hay making.

“Our goal is to send the hand hoe to the museum and liberate the African farmer from the backbreaking drudgery of tilling the land by hand.”
